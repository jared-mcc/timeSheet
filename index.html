This time sheet application is meant to be a simple alternative to paper-and-pencil time sheets.

No data is stored by me, but is stored on the device and browser you are currently using.

As such, your time sheet will only be accessible from whatever you are browsing with right now.

To Send Data:

I am working on a literal "send" button that should be in place soon.

Until then, a screenshot is the easiest way to transport your time sheet.

Start by entering the first or last day of the pay period.
